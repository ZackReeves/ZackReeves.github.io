Music Synthesizer

Project information

This project required writing embedded sofware for a music synthesizer keyboard in a group of 4. The end product included a variety of features, including:

  • Polyphonic sound - playing multpile notes simultaneously.
  • Automatic configuaration of multiple keyboards, allowing them to be attached and detached from each other to span multiple octaves.
  • Support for 4 waveforms; square, triangular, sawtooth, and sinusoidal.
  • Volume, octave and waveform control via 3 seperate knobs.
  • Analog pitch bend and tremolo controls.

Threads and interupts were utilized to ensure tasks were executed within their maximum time intervals. This required a thread safe implementation designed with mutexes, semaphores and atomic functions.
